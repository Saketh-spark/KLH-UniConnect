## ğŸ†˜ Troubleshooting

### Issue: Event not appearing to students
**Solution**: Make sure you **published** the event. Events start in "Draft" and are only visible to faculty. Click the eye icon to publish.

### Issue: Student registration not showing
**Solution**: Refresh the page or wait a few seconds. Real-time updates may take a moment to sync.

### Issue: Can't approve a club
**Solution**: Only faculty can approve clubs. Ensure your role is set to "Faculty". Contact admin if needed.

### Issue: Attendance marks not saving
**Solution**: Check your internet connection. Try marking attendance again. Ensure backend is running.
